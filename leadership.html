Leadership Team

Meet the dedicated office bearers guiding our initiative forward. Our leaders are elected by members through a democratic process and serve a fixed term of one year.

Chairman

Provides executive leadership to the Group, presides over all meetings of the Executive Committee and General Meetings, organizes meeting agendas, encourages participation by all members, and represents the Group at external meetings.

Damaris Mokeira

Vice Chairperson

Strength is not in standing alone, but in lifting each other up. When one of us rises, we all rise.

Millicent Kerubo

Treasurer / Secretary

Manages all Group finances, maintains proper books of accounts, handles correspondence, and keeps minutes of all meetings.
