About Us

Who We Are

Glamorous Care Initiative (GCI) is a dedicated community group committed to making a positive impact. We believe in the power of unity and the profound difference that collective care can bring to society.

Our Vision

A society where everyone experiences love, care, and equal opportunities to thrive.

Our Mission

To uplift the marginalized, support the vulnerable, and foster a strong, connected community.
